North Invest - Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2026

Introduction

North Invest ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

North Invest is designed to work entirely offline. All data you enter in the app (income records, expenses, invoices, and salary information) is stored locally on your device only.

Data Storage

All financial data you enter is stored exclusively on your device in local storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Is not backed up to any cloud service by the app

Data Export

The app provides a CSV export feature that allows you to export your data. When you use this feature, the exported file is created on your device and you control where it is shared or saved.

Third-Party Services

North Invest does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
